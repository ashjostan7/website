ABOUT ME

"Enabling Analysis of Human Movement and Human-Equipment Interaction using Technology."

I’ve recently graduated as a Sports Engineer from the Centre for Sports Engineering and Research (CSER), Sheffield Hallam University. During my term time as a master’s student I worked alongside New Balance to report on "Feasibility of using pose estimation algorithms to study lower leg kinematics.” During my Computer Science undergraduate studies at CHRIST University I had the opportunity to collaborate with Sports Authority of India, Bangalore to analyse push off angle of sprinters using Open Pose.

I grew up in Bangalore , India running on roads and tracks, flying kites , spinning tops and swinging a cricket bat. After completing my computer science and engineering undergraduate studies at Christ University , Bangalore, I moved to UK to study my Masters in Sports Engineering at Sheffield Hallam University.

Pursuing a career in developing technology and enabling people in sport to hone the benefits technology.
